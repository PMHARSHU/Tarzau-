About Tarazu

Built for Real India,
Real Shops

Tarazu App is a free, offline-ready toolkit designed specifically for shopkeepers, kirana store owners, merchants, and everyday buyers across India who need instant, error-free calculations during busy store hours.

Figuring out how many grams to give a customer for their ₹15 when sugar costs ₹40/kg shouldn't require a calculator app, a pen, or mental math under pressure. Tarazu solves this in real-time as you type.

Beyond weight, it covers EMI planning for electronics and appliance sellers, SIP return projections for financially-aware users, percentage & grade calculations for students — all in one place, always free.
